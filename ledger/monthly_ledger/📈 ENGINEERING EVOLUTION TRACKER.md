
# Description :
Every week /2 weeks

# Prompt: 
Generate Engineering Evolution Snapshot.


# Template:
## 🧠 Thinking Maturity Shift
- Compared to previous snapshots, what cognitive upgrade happened?
- Are problems being framed more abstractly?
- Is there less noise in reasoning?

## 🏗 Architecture Depth Level
- Are we thinking in features?
- Modules?
- Abstractions?
- Systems?
- Platforms?

Classify current level explicitly.

## 🔁 Recurrent Weak Patterns
- What mistakes or thinking traps are repeating?
- Where do I still overcomplicate?
- Where do I avoid abstraction?

## ⚡ Decision Velocity
- Am I hesitating more or less?
- Are decisions backed by principles or emotion?

## 📊 Complexity Handling Capacity
- What level of system complexity can I now hold mentally?
- Has that threshold increased?

## 🎯 Evolution Direction
- What kind of engineer am I becoming?
  (Builder / Architect / System Designer / Platform Thinker)
